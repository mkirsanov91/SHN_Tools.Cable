About

Developed by Shnabel — a digital tools initiative for MEP engineering workflows. Built to make everyday calculations faster, clearer, and more accessible for the project office.

Tools included

Cable Tray Fill Calculator — fill percentage per IEC 60364, NEC, BS 7671, with gravity-based visual cross-section and PDF export.

Cable Pulling Calculator — tension and sidewall pressure analysis with route profile and load scenarios. Full PDF report with jsPDF.

Conduit Fill Calculator — conduit sizing per Israel Electrical Code (Hok HaHashmal, regulation 18, chapter 3). Formula D = √n × d × 1.6. Supports PVC, PP, HDPE and steel conduits in metric and imperial sizes. MV cables 22kV and 33kV (N2XSY / NA2XSY per IEC 60502-2). Multi-conduit project mode with combined PDF export.

EMI Vertical Separation Calculator — vertical separation between power and communication trays based on the EN 50174-2 approach, with cable-to-cable and tray-to-tray results, engineering recommendation, visualization and PDF export.
